Fighter

Fighters are versatile warriors. They can use nearly any type of weapon and adapt to almost any situation on a battlefield. A Fighter copd be a disciplined soldier or a dishonorable bandit.

Bonuses

  • +1 Constitution
  • +2 Melee Weapon

    Skills

    Melee Weapon

      Ability: Strength
      Attack or defend with a melee weapon.

    Melee Unarmed

      Ability: Strength
      Attack or defend using one's hands or feet.

    Ranged Weapon

      Ability: Dexterity
      Attack or defend using a ranged weapon.

    Magic (Combat)

      Ability: Intelligence or Wisdom (use highest)
      Attack or defend using magic or supernatural powers.

    Magic (Utility)

      Ability: Intelligence or Wisdom (use highest)
      Use magic or supernatural powers for purposes other than attacking and defending.

    Agility

      Ability: Dexterity

    Will

      Ability: Wisdom

    Athletics

      Ability: Strength

    Charm

      Ability: Charisma
      Befriend or flatter a creature.

    Deceive

      Ability: Charisma
      Lie to or mislead a creature.

    Endurance

      Ability: Constitution
      Endure through exposure to weather, starvation, disease and other environmental effects.

    Intimidate

      Ability: Charisma
      Threaten or frighten a creature.

    Perceive

      Ability: Wisdom
      Search for notable objects and creatures in the enviroment.

    Stealth

      Ability: Dexterity
      Hide from a creature and conceal one's presense.

    Steal

      Ability: Dexterity
      Pick pockets and circumvent security measures.

    Entertain

      Ability: Charisma
      Impress or please a creature through artistic performance.

    Knowledge: Science and Mathematics

      Ability: Intelligence
      Knowledge of all areas of scientific study, including mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology and geology.

    Knowledge: Culture and History

      Ability: Wisdom
      Knowledge of the people, places, cpture and history of the world.

    Knowledge: Magic and the Supernatual

      Ability: Intelligence or Wisdom (use highest)
      Knowledge of all forms of magic and supernatural phenomena.

    Knowledge: Military and Tactics

      Ability: Intelligence
      Knowledge of military strategies and tactics.

    Knowledge: Crafting and Engineering

      Ability: Wisdom
      Knowledge of designing and creating useful things.

    Knowledge: Medicine

      Wisdom
      Knowledge of treating injuries and diseases.

    Knowledge: Religion

      Ability: Wisdom
      Knowledge of the religions and deities of the world.

    Knowledge: Survival and Sustenance

      Ability: Wisdon
      Knowlege of survival techniques for meeting basic needs, including hunting, cooking, sanitation and shelter.
